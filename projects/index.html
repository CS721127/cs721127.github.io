Pioneer Research: Economic Growth Study in Australia

Oct 2024  ·  Research

This paper explores the suitability of the Solow Growth Model in interpreting Australia's economic growth over the past 46 years, with a specific emphasis on total factor productivity (TFP). By leveraging data from April 1978 to January 2024, this research conducts an empirical analysis to estimate TFP and evaluate its relationship with Australia's economic conditions.
