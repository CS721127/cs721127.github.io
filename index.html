Why I Made This Website

As a student, I have a lot of fun in my life. However, there are too many things to remember all of them. I am a person who misses past memories and will often go through my past times to refresh my mind with those pure, lovely memories, as well as the importance of learning from the past.

Therefore, this website is a collection of my activities — valuable to my memory and that I will look back on again. It will always remind me of something important, either lessons or greetings. Hopefully, some pieces of my activities will remind you of your past memories too.

Take care, and wish you the best brighter future!
